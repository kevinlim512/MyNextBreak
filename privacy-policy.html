Privacy Policy

App: Countdown App
Last updated: 2025-10-20

This Privacy Policy explains how Countdown App ("the App") handles information. We take your privacy seriously and aim to be clear and concise.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. Your countdowns and preferences are stored locally on your device and are not shared with us.

Third‑Party Services

The App does not integrate third‑party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. If this changes in the future, this page will be updated prior to any such integration, and you will be provided with appropriate notice and controls.

Data Security

Because data remains on your device, please ensure your device is kept secure and up to date. We do not have access to your data and cannot recover it if it is lost from your device.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will address the issue promptly.
